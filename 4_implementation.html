IMPLEMENTATION

After the development phase, the socioenactive system is implemented in a real physical space. This phase involves designing and implementing socioenactive spaces and environments.

Socioenactive Spaces

The implementation of socioactive spaces involves linking all the technological components in a physical space in real-time. In this step, physical spaces are designed and implemented with the technological components of the socioenactive system linked among them through the internet in real-time.

Socioenactive Environment

This phase involves designing and implementing socioenactive spaces, including the narrative and flux of activities with participants/people who will experience the system.
